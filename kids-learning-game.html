😊 Learning Good Manners

👋

Say Please & Thank You

Always use magic words! When you want something, say "Please" and when someone helps you, say "Thank you!"

💡 Remember: Magic words make everyone happy!
😊

Smile & Be Friendly

A smile is a gift! Smile at people, say hello, and make new friends. Being kind and friendly makes the world brighter.

💡 Your smile can make someone's day better!
👂

Listen Carefully

When someone is talking, look at them and listen carefully. Don't interrupt. Show that you care about what they say.

💡 Good listeners are great friends!
🙏

Say Sorry & Forgive

Everyone makes mistakes! When you do something wrong, say "I'm sorry" and try to do better. Forgive others too!

💡 Sorry makes things right again!
🤝

Help Others

Look for ways to help! Help your friends, family, and even strangers. Sharing is caring, and helping feels wonderful!

💡 Helping others makes you a superhero!
🧹

Keep Things Clean

Wash your hands before eating and after playing. Keep your room tidy. Taking care of your space shows respect.

💡 Clean hands and spaces = Happy places!
🎂

Be Patient

Good things take time! Wait your turn, be patient with others, and don't rush. Patience shows you're mature and kind.

💡 Patience is a super power!
🚫

Never Hurt Others

Use kind words and gentle hands. Never hit, push, or say mean things. Everyone deserves to be treated with respect.

💡 Words and hands should always be kind!

✨ Congratulations! ✨

You're learning to be a good person!

Keep practicing these good manners every day! 🌟
